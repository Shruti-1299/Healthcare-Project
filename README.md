# Healthcare-Data-Analysis (Interactive Dashboard creation using Excel & PowerBI)
## Project Objective
To analyze healthcare data and gain insights into patient demographics, medical conditions, hospital admissions, and treatment outcomes.

## Questions (KPIs)
Q.  How many total patients, doctors, and hospitals are represented in the dataset?
Q.  What is the average billing amount across all patients?
Q.  What is the distribution of patients across admission types (Elective, Emergency, Urgent)?
Q.  Which admission type has the highest number of patients?
Q.  How are patients distributed across different age groups?
Q.  Which age group has the highest patient count?
Q.  What is the gender-wise distribution of patients?
Q. Which insurance provider covers the most patients?
